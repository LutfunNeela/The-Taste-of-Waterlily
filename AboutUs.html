Our Authenticity

The Taste of WaterLily was created by and for Bangladeshi immigrants initially.

Our mission is to spread the joy of good Bangladeshi food and our Bangladeshi culture of Hospitality.

Our chef's have been practicing their skills over a span of 10 years in the hospitality industry of Bangladesh. They have honed their skill and techniques from true masters of Bangladeshi Cusine.

Our Food

Our Menu ranges from regular homemade food that average people of Bangladesh have on a daily basis to exclusive Bangladeshi platters.

Bangladeshi cusine has one of the few multi-course traditions in South Asia. The cusine is the perfect balance of light spiced and rich food. The staple food of the country is fish & rice. So we have tried to incorporate that into our menu. Please come & enjoy our hospitality.

Travel to Bangladesh without traveling with our exclusive food experience.

Our Ingredients

We prepare our food using premium ingredients. We source our ingredients from premium sources. We have a herb garden in-house.

The herb garden contains different kind of herbs. The mint, corianders, basil, chillies, and other herbs are sources from our herb garden organically.

Halal Certified

Our food is Zabiha Halal. Our Staff maintains high standards of professional standards to maintain your comfort and your faith.
