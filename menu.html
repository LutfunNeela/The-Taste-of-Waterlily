Our Food

Our menu reflects authentic Bangladeshi flavors. From the pungent sharp aroma of mustard oil, to the sweetness of nolen gur, from the umami of dried fishes to the richness of authentic Kacchi our food has it all.

What Should you expect?

You might have heard about other Asian food and culture. Let us open an window to Bangladeshi culture and food. You can expect to find authentic Bangladeshi flavors. We offer traditional food and flavors that are native to Bangladesh.
